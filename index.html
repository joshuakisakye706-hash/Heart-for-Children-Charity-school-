Who We Are

Heart for Children Charity School was founded in 2025 with one dream: to give every child—no matter their background—a safe place to learn, grow, and build a bright future.

We started with a small group of volunteers teaching under wooden building using donated books. Today, we serve hundreds of children with love, care, and quality education.

Our Mission

To provide accessible education, emotional support, meals, and community programs that empower children and help families break the cycle of poverty.
